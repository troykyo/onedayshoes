Design it Yourself

Want to make your own sneakers? Want to design your own sneakers? ONEDAYS are made just for you. We designed ONEDAYS to be easy to customize. Our patterns have a High Top and Low Top ready to go. We designed them to be simple so that you can remake them to be uniquely yours. We are in the middle of our Kickstarter Campaign right now. If we reach our extended funding goals we will soon have lessons on pattern making with the simple ONEDAY patterns. You can download the pattern for your size right now and get started right now.

Picking your Material

Sure, you can buy new material for your ONEDAYS. But you can recycle something too. Have an old pair of jeans? How about a leather coat from a second hand store? Want to hand weave sholaces? Great! Materials are everywhere. Just look around you.

Want to laser cut your pattern at your local Fablab or maybe order leather from Ponoko? No problem. We have laser cutting and etching templates available so you can digitaly fabricate your uppers as well.

The Soles

Our Premium soles are made with the finest Polyurathane to be flexible yet resistent. We chose to manufacture the soles in Europe so that the are held to high quality and ecological standards.
