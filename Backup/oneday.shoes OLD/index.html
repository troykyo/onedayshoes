ONEDAY is a great shoemaking kit. What's inside?

Thread

Attaching the Soles to Uppers is serious business. That is why we include some great shoe thread.

Soles

Soles are the hardest part. These soles are some of the best out there.

Pattern

Included is a great basic pattern that fits the soles perfectly and can be easily modified.

Instructions

We made the ONEDAY shoe kit to make these things easy. Inside there is a great pair of soles, the perfect thread to attach then and a great pattern that can make lots of different types of shoes. All you need to add is the upper material, an insole, shoelaces and one day of your time to create your own pair of ONEDAYS.

ONEDAY Shoes

ONEDAY started as a workshop at SLEM, the worlds leading Footwear Innovation institute. Shoe Designer Roderick Peters meet Wearable Technology Designer Troy Nachtigall and together they created ONEDAY Shoes to bring the joy of shoemaking to everyone.

Roderick Pieters

Graduate of Arnhem Fashion Accademy, Roderick Designs shoes for some of the newest and coolest brands out there. He travels the world designing shoes wherever he goes.

SLEM

SLEM is an international innovation and education institute for footwear. SLEM brings together footwear innovators from all over the world to make some of tomorrows most interesting footwear innovations.

Troy Nachtigall

Graduate of the Fashion Institute of Technology in NYC. Troy works between Italy and the Netherlands. Troy Nachtigall is a Wearable Technology designer who is fascinated by sensing the human body and has worked with many companies and universities in Wearable research.
